WHO WE ARE

Here at Vancro Integrated Interpreting Services, our interpreter-led directorate manages a cohort of highly skilled interpreters by remaining true to our three pillars:

Great interpreters

Exceptional Service

Equal Access

Great interpreters

VIIS searches for the most qualified interpreters and matches only the best fit to each of the contracts we serve. Being interpreters ourselves, we understand the ins and outs of the profession and navigate our way to selecting appropriate interpreters for every assignment. We search for interpreters that have a wide breadth of experience and possess unique skills that show their ability in producing high quality interpretations. We maintain quality assurance by utilizing our in-depth Assessment, Screening, and Selection process. By fully vetting our interpreters, including their experience and skill, we can be confident in our assignment of interpreters to Designated Interpreting Teams.

Exceptional Service

We strive for excellence in all we do when providing services to our customers and clients. From honoring stakeholder preference when it comes to interpreter requests, to requesting feedback post-assignment, we take “Respect for Consumers” to the heart. We are ever-evaluating our processes in service - looking for ways to enhance the experience of our consumers. We maintain an open, constant line of communication with all stakeholders when providing services as it is detrimental to the success of each assignment. Going above and beyond is a standard in how VIIS manages contracts and how we continue to serve our customers and clients.

Equal Access

Most importantly, our goal is to provide equal access to each of our consumers. Students, professionals, and patients are just some of the types of consumers that we serve, however we like to categorize them simply as: people. At VIIS, we believe that all people have the right to communicate, to receive services, and to live equally. It is our ethical responsibility to do everything we can to make that happen!
