WHY ARE WE DIFFERENT?

Vancro Integrated Interpreting Services service philosophy is what separates our firm from others. At Vancro, we pride ourselves on honoring Deaf professional preference as well as local interpreter rates while providing exceptional provision nationwide.


Click below to learn more on how Vancro’s service philosophy comes to life:

MANAGEMENT TEAM:

Vancro takes a unique approach to managing each contract we are awarded. Our quality assurance is tantamount to exceptional interpreter provision. Each of our contracts will have direct contact to leadership at any time.

Vancro is proud of our interpreter selection process. We ensure that every interpreter is a great match for the assignment. We take vetting of interpreters seriously, as well as consistency and client interpreter preference.

INTERPRETER ASSESSMENT

Every interpreter selected by Vancro must undergo a thorough assessment process as a safeguard in ensuring the highest quality of service provision. Vancro reviews interpreter credentials and work experience and performance against information provided in resumes and reference letters. Each interpreter is tested, screened and assessed to the highest standards of professionalism in American Sign Language Interpreting.

INTERPRETER MANAGEMENT SYSTEMS

Vancro uses internal high performance Interpreting Management Systems used for scheduling, assigning, and confirming interpreters. This system allows for site specific requestors to select preferred interpreters and allows both interpreters and our customers to manage scheduling times.

Mentorship/CEU's For Interpreters

Vancro recognizes the importance of continued growth and professional development. Vancro continually offers workshops to assist in developing added proficiency in areas our interpreters would like to develop. Additionally, Vancro believes in mentorship and giving back to our interpreting and Deaf communities. Have you completed an ITP but aren’t yet ready to dive into the professional world? Come see what mentorship opportunities Vancro has available to meet your needs and get you ready for whatever your goal may be!
