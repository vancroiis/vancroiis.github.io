WHO WE SERVE

Our unique approach to hiring and booking interpreters allows for the best experience for our 3 key stakeholders: the people who request interpreters, the people who use interpreters and the professional interpreters we work with. Vancro provides exceptional remote interpreting. We can use your preferred software or ours. We have highly qualified interpreters who are adept with Skype, MS Teams, Zoom, FaceTime, WebX and our own Internal Remote Portal. We provide interpreters to:

Federal Agencies

State agencies and departments

Colleges and Universities

Conferences

K-12 Education

Ceremonies - Weddings, Funerals, Graduations

Commercial businesses – on the job training, orientation,
work staff meetings, etc.
